Paraná, Londrina

In the 1920s, British investors from the Paraná Plantations Limited arrived in the region. They were struck by the thick, silvery mist that often covered the rolling hills and the fertile red soil. It reminded them so much of the foggy atmosphere of London that they named the settlement Londrina as a tribute to their capital.
